About us

Welcome to Deducation, the premier platform for creating a vast network within the higher educational sphere. Our platform is designed to cater to four distinct groups of users: students, universities, professors, and employers.



Students

We provide an invaluable opportunity for students to make informed decisions about their educational journey. Our unique ML-tool allows students to explore and compare various educational programs offered by different universities. By considering their interests, skills, and hobbies, students can choose the university that aligns best with their aspirations.

Universities

We empower universities to enhance their educational programs and services. Our platform enables universities to compare their offerings with those of other institutions, facilitating the identification of areas for development. Additionally, universities can forge valuable partnerships with employers, opening doors to potential contracts and collaborations.

Professors

We foster a vibrant community where professors can expand their professional networks and unlock new opportunities. Professors have the ability to connect with colleagues, gain accreditation from prestigious universities, and exchange their expertise. Furthermore, they can offer private tutoring services, creating a platform for knowledge-sharing and personal growth.

Employers

We simplify the hiring process for employers by providing tailored suggestions based on required skills and knowledge. Our platform connects employers with talented individuals from diverse educational backgrounds, ensuring the selection of qualified candidates who meet their specific requirements.

At Deducation, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the higher educational landscape by connecting individuals and institutions, fostering collaboration, and facilitating informed decision-making.Join us today and embark on a transformative educational journey.
